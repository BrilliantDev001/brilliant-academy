
You're to create a mini school portal for "Geegstack University"

The portal should have the following pages:
 1. Create Account
 2. Login
 3. Results
 4. Fees Payment
 5. Account creation success page.
 6. Student's profile page

The pages should have the following features;
General; School logo and name at the top of the page.
Footer: school copyright, current year.

Use color codes: #4ad and white

 1. Create account: A form that collects necessary students information such as: Name, assigned matric, date of birth, gender(drop-down), email, password, and bio. Note that the inputs should have correct types and all fields are required. Student should be taken to the Account creation success page upon submission

 2. Login: A form that collects email and password. Both should be required. Upon submission, user should be redirected to Profile page.

 3. Results: A tabular form of students courses and the result for the past semester. There should be at least six courses.

 4. Fees Payment: should show student past payment and a form to make new payment. Form should allow them to choose the payment reason: school fees, hostel fee, technology fee.

 5. Account creation success; A success icon, success message and Link to Profile page.

 6. Student profile page: Student image, all students details and link to fees payment and logout link. Logout link should direct to login page

Above are the required information. You're to apply your creative knowledge to produce the best result.
